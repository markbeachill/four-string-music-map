The Ukulele Fool

Lisa, The Ukulele Fool, creates chord charts and lessons designed to build practical ukulele skills. Her teaching includes beginner songs, chord changes, strumming, chucking and play-alongs, presented with an emphasis on making steady progress through enjoyable repertoire.
