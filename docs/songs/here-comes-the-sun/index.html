Here Comes the Sun

Associated with The Beatles · 1969

George Harrison wrote and sang "Here Comes the Sun" for the Beatles' 1969 album Abbey Road, recording it at EMI Studios that summer. It is now the most-streamed Beatles song on Spotify.
