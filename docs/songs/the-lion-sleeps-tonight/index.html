The Lion Sleeps Tonight

Associated with The Tokens · 1961

South African singer Solomon Linda wrote and recorded "Mbube" with his group the Evening Birds in 1939, the first African record to sell over 100,000 copies. Folk artists including Pete Seeger popularized it as "Wimoweh," and songwriter George David Weiss added English lyrics for the Tokens' 1961 hit "The Lion Sleeps Tonight"; Linda's family only received a fair share of royalties in 2006, decades after his death.

Genre
Pop / doo-wop
