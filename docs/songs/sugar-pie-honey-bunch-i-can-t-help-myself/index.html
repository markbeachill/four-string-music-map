(Sugar Pie Honey Bunch) I Can't Help Myself

Associated with Four Tops · 1965

Motown's Holland–Dozier–Holland team wrote "I Can't Help Myself" for the Four Tops in 1965; Lamont Dozier said the "Sugar pie, honey bunch" hook came from memories of his grandfather flirting with his grandmother. It topped the Billboard Hot 100 for two weeks that June.

Genre
Soul / Motown
