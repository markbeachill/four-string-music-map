Ain't No Sunshine

Associated with Bill Withers · 1971

Bill Withers wrote and recorded "Ain't No Sunshine" for his 1971 debut album Just As I Am, inspired by the film Days of Wine and Roses. The repeated "I know" section was meant as a placeholder for unfinished lyrics, but producer Booker T. Jones and the musicians kept it in the final mix.
