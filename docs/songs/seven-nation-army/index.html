Seven Nation Army

Associated with The White Stripes · 2003

Jack White came up with the riff for "Seven Nation Army" during a 2002 soundcheck at a Melbourne pub and lowered its pitch with a guitar effects pedal to sound like a bass. Released in 2003 on the White Stripes' album Elephant, its stomping riff became one of the most widely chanted hooks in sports arenas worldwide.
