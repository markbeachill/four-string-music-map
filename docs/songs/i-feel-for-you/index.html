I Feel for You

Associated with Prince · 1979

Prince wrote "I Feel for You" for his 1979 self-titled album after Patrice Rushen turned it down. Chaka Khan's 1984 cover, featuring a rap by Melle Mel and harmonica from Stevie Wonder, became the far bigger hit and won Prince a Grammy for Best R&B Song.
