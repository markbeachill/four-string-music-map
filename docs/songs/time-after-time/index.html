Time After Time

Associated with Cyndi Lauper · 1983

Cyndi Lauper and Rob Hyman wrote "Time After Time" at a piano in early 1983, both going through difficult relationships at the time; Lauper took the title from a listing for the film Time After Time in TV Guide. Released in 1984, it became her only US No. 1 single.
