Itchycoo Park

Associated with Small Faces · 1967

Steve Marriott and Ronnie Lane wrote "Itchycoo Park" for the Small Faces, recording it at Olympic Studios in early 1967. Engineer George Chkiantz's use of flanging on the track made it one of the first pop hits to feature the effect.

Genre
Psychedelic rock
