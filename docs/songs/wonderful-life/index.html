Wonderful Life

Associated with Black · 1987

Colin Vearncombe, performing as Black, wrote "Wonderful Life" in about ten minutes during a period of homelessness and hardship, later saying the title was meant ironically. First released in 1986 on an independent label, a 1987 reissue became a top-ten hit across Europe.
