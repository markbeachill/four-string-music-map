Sway

Associated with Pérez Prado · 1953

Pablo Beltrán Ruiz composed "¿Quién Será?" in 1953, a mambo-bolero later given new English lyrics by Norman Gimbel and popularized as "Sway" by Dean Martin in 1954. Pérez Prado's 1960 instrumental version brought the tune back to its Latin roots for a new audience.
