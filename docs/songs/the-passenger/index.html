The Passenger

Associated with Iggy Pop · 1977

Guitarist Ricky Gardiner wrote the music for "The Passenger" after being struck by a chord sequence while walking through an orchard, then brought it to Iggy Pop in Berlin. Pop's lyrics, partly inspired by a Jim Morrison poem and by riding Berlin's S-Bahn with David Bowie, were recorded for the 1977 album Lust for Life.
