Hey Good Lookin'

Associated with Hank Williams · 1951

Hank Williams wrote "Hey, Good Lookin'" in a few minutes during a tour flight in 1951 and recorded it that March at Castle Studio in Nashville. It became his fifth No. 1 country hit, though its title and some phrasing echo Cole Porter's 1942 song of the same name.
