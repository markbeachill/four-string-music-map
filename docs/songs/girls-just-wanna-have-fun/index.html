Girls Just Wanna Have Fun

Associated with Cyndi Lauper · 1983

Robert Hazard wrote "Girls Just Want to Have Fun" in 1979 from a male perspective. Producer Rick Chertoff brought it to Cyndi Lauper, who reworked it into the feminist pop anthem released in 1983, and its music video passed one billion YouTube views in 2022.

Genre
