I'll Fly Away

Associated with Albert E. Brumley

Albert E. Brumley began "I'll Fly Away" in 1928 while picking cotton, humming an old ballad about wings of an angel, and took roughly three years to finish it before its 1932 publication. It has since become one of the most recorded gospel songs of all time.
