Leaving on a Jet Plane

Associated with Peter, Paul and Mary · 1967

John Denver wrote "Leaving on a Jet Plane" (originally titled "Babe I Hate to Go") during a 1966 airport layover and recorded it himself for his 1969 album Rhymes & Reasons. Peter, Paul and Mary's 1969 cover became the more famous version and their only US No. 1 hit.
