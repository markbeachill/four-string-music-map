Take Me Home, Country Roads

Associated with John Denver · 1971

A familiar John Denver song with a repeating chord vocabulary and clear phrases. It is a useful early full-song project for steady strumming, singing and transposition.

Genre
Country / folk-pop
Ukulele key
C
Chords
C, F, G, Am (common ukulele arrangement)
