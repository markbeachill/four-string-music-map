Stand by Me

Associated with Ben E. King · 1961

A repeating four-chord soul classic that is ideal for practising an unbroken groove. Its circular progression also makes it a strong first example for hearing harmonic function.

Genre
Soul / doo-wop
Feel
Steady soul groove
Ukulele key
C
Chords
C, Am, F, G (common ukulele arrangement)
Progression
I–vi–IV–V
