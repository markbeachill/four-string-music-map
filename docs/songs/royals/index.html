Royals

Associated with Lorde · 2013

Lorde wrote the lyrics to "Royals" in about half an hour in mid-2012, taking the title from a photo of baseball player George Brett signing autographs for the Kansas City Royals. She and producer Joel Little recorded it in a week at Auckland's Golden Age Studios, and it won two Grammy Awards in 2014.
