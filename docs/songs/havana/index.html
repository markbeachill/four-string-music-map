Havana

Associated with Camila Cabello · 2017

Camila Cabello wrote "Havana" with Pharrell Williams and Ali Tamposi, drawing on her Cuban heritage; she has said it took roughly five months and seven different versions to finish. Released in 2017 as the lead single from her debut solo album, it became her first US No. 1 hit.
