Hotel California

Associated with Eagles · 1976

Don Felder wrote the music for "Hotel California", with lyrics by Don Henley and Glenn Frey; the Eagles released it as a single in February 1977 from the album of the same name. It won the Grammy for Record of the Year in 1978.
