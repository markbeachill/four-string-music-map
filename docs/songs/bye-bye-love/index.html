Bye Bye Love

Associated with The Everly Brothers · 1957

Felice and Boudleaux Bryant wrote "Bye Bye Love", and it was turned down by roughly 30 other acts before The Everly Brothers recorded it in January 1957. It became the duo's first hit, reaching No. 2 on the Billboard pop chart and No. 1 on the country chart.

Genre
Rock and roll / country
