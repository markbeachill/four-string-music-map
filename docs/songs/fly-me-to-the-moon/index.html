Fly Me to the Moon

Associated with Frank Sinatra · 1954

Bart Howard wrote the song in 1954 under the title "In Other Words"; it was first recorded that year by Kaye Ballard. Frank Sinatra's 1964 recording with Count Basie's orchestra popularized the now-familiar title and became closely associated with the Apollo Moon missions.

Genre
Jazz standard
