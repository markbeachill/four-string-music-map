Walking on Sunshine

Associated with Katrina and the Waves · 1983

Kimberley Rew wrote "Walking on Sunshine" for Katrina and the Waves' 1983 album of the same name, later saying it was simply "a piece of simple fun" rather than inspired by any single event. A re-recorded version became the band's breakthrough US and UK hit in 1985.

Genre
