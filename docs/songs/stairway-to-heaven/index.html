Stairway to Heaven

Associated with Led Zeppelin · 1971

Jimmy Page began composing "Stairway to Heaven" at the remote Welsh cottage Bron-Yr-Aur, with Robert Plant adding lyrics during sessions for Led Zeppelin IV in 1971. Never released as a single, it became one of rock's most played songs regardless.
