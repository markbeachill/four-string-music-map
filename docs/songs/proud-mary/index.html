Proud Mary

Associated with Creedence Clearwater Revival · 1969

John Fogerty wrote "Proud Mary" within an hour of receiving his discharge from the Army Reserve in 1968, saying it was the first song he was ever proud of. Released by Creedence Clearwater Revival in January 1969, it reached No. 2 on the Billboard Hot 100, and Ike & Tina Turner's 1971 cover later won a Grammy.
