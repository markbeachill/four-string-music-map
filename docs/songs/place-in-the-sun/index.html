Place in the Sun

Associated with Stevie Wonder · 1966

Ronald Miller and Bryan Wells wrote "A Place in the Sun" for Stevie Wonder, who recorded it in 1966 at age 16. Its hopeful, socially conscious lyric marked an early step from his child-star pop hits toward more substantive songwriting, and it reached No. 9 on the Billboard pop chart.

Genre
Soul / Motown
