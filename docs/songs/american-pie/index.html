American Pie

Associated with Don McLean · 1971

Don McLean wrote and recorded "American Pie" in 1971. Its extended, allusive lyric traces American music and culture through the 1950s and 60s, opening with the 1959 plane crash that killed Buddy Holly — the event McLean later called "the day the music died."
