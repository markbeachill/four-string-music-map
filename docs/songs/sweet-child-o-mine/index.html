Sweet Child O' Mine

Associated with Guns N' Roses · 1987

Guns N' Roses wrote "Sweet Child o' Mine" almost by accident in 1987 after Slash began noodling a "circus" riff during a jam session; Axl Rose set it to a poem he had written for his then-girlfriend Erin Everly. It became the band's only US No. 1 single.
