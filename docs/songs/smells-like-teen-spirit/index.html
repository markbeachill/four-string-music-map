Smells Like Teen Spirit

Associated with Nirvana · 1991

Kurt Cobain wrote "Smells Like Teen Spirit" shortly before recording Nevermind in 1991, aiming for what he called "the ultimate pop song" inspired by the Pixies' loud-quiet dynamics. Its title came from a friend's graffiti referencing a deodorant brand, which Cobain misread as a revolutionary slogan.

Genre
Grunge / alternative rock
