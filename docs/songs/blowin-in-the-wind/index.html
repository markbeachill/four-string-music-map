Blowin' in the Wind

Associated with Bob Dylan · 1963

Bob Dylan wrote "Blowin' in the Wind" in 1962 and recorded it for his 1963 album The Freewheelin' Bob Dylan. Peter, Paul and Mary's cover, released weeks later, became the bigger hit and helped establish the song as a civil rights and folk-revival anthem.
