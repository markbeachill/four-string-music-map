Folsom Prison Blues

Associated with Johnny Cash · 1955

Johnny Cash wrote and recorded "Folsom Prison Blues" in 1955 at Sun Studio in Memphis. Its melody closely echoed Gordon Jenkins's 1953 "Crescent City Blues", and Cash later paid Jenkins a settlement after a lawsuit over the resemblance.
