House of the Rising Sun

Associated with Traditional

"House of the Rising Sun" is an American folk song of uncertain authorship, first collected in Appalachia in the 1930s. The Animals' hard-edged 1964 arrangement, recorded in a single take, topped the charts on both sides of the Atlantic; the arranging credit went to keyboardist Alan Price alone, a lasting source of tension in the band.
